## Design Context

### Users
This site serves readers, recruiters, peers, and potential collaborators who are evaluating Adrian's product leadership, writing, and hands-on AI work. Most visits are quick scan sessions on phones or laptops where the job is to understand credibility fast and reach the right destination without friction.

### Brand Personality
Warm, precise, grounded. The voice should feel human, editorial, and quietly confident rather than flashy or startup-generic.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep the current light-mode, paper-and-ink editorial feel. Responsive changes should preserve the calm, literary tone while making mobile interaction feel intentional, compact, and touch-friendly.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the brand quiet and confident.
2. Make mobile navigation and touch interaction effortless.
3. Tighten spacing on small screens before shrinking meaning.
4. Remove scaffolding that reads like a mockup instead of published work.
5. Preserve canonical information architecture and long-lived routes.
